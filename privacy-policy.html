Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/10/2025 · Last Updated: 08/10/2025

Introduction

GoCrewIT ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our field service management platform and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title
  • Business Information: Company details, tax identification numbers, billing addresses
  • Job Site Data: Project details, work orders, customer information, photos, signatures, documents
  • Communication Data: Messages, support requests, feedback, and correspondence
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through third-party payment processors (we do not store full credit card numbers)

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Service, features used, time spent, clicks, and navigation patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when using mobile features (with your permission)
  • Log Data: Server logs, error reports, performance data, and security logs
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze usage

Information from Third Parties

  • Integration Partners: Data from connected accounting, CRM, or other business software
  • Social Media: Information from social media platforms if you connect your accounts
  • Business Partners: Information from contractors, suppliers, or other business contacts you work with

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

Provide and Improve Our Service

  • Operate and maintain the GoCrewIT platform
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Develop new features and improve existing functionality
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

Communication

  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Provide marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Send important announcements about our Service

Business Operations

  • Analyze usage patterns and user behavior
  • Generate business insights and analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and interests

Marketing and Promotion

  • Send promotional emails and newsletters (with opt-out options)
  • Display targeted advertisements
  • Conduct market research and surveys
  • Analyze marketing campaign effectiveness

How We Share Your Information

We Share Information With:

Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate our business, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers (Google Cloud)
  • Payment processors (Stripe)
  • Email service providers (Gmail)
  • Analytics providers (Squarespace and Google Analytics)
  • Customer support tools (Google)

Business Partners:

  • Integration partners (accounting software, CRM systems)
  • Contractors and suppliers you work with through our platform
  • Other users within your organization or project teams

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law, regulation, or court order
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent fraud or security threats
  • In connection with law enforcement investigations

Business Transactions:

  • In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • During due diligence processes
  • To potential investors or buyers

We Do NOT Share Information:

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share sensitive business data without your explicit consent
  • We do not provide customer lists to competitors

Data Security and Protection

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices including:

Technical Safeguards:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication options
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Automated backup systems with encryption

Administrative Safeguards:

  • Employee background checks and confidentiality agreements
  • Regular security training for staff
  • Limited access controls based on job responsibilities
  • Incident response procedures

Physical Safeguards:

  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Biometric access controls
  • Environmental protection systems
  • Redundant power and network systems

Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Provide details about the incident and remediation steps
  • Offer credit monitoring or other protective services if appropriate

Data Retention and Deletion

Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 7 years after closure for legal compliance
  • Job Site Data: Retained as long as needed for business purposes, typically 7 years
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 2 years for security and analytics purposes
  • Support Communications: Retained for 5 years for quality assurance

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information by:

  • Contacting our support team at admin@gocrewit.com
  • Using account settings to delete specific data
  • Closing your account (some data may be retained for legal compliance)

We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements.
